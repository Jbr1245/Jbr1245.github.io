My Goals

My main goal is to graduate ahead of schedule with a strong academic record and meaningful project experience. I plan to pursue internships that allow me to apply mechanical design, simulation, and manufacturing techniques to real-world engineering problems.

I'm passionate about developing skills in CAD design, welding, and mechanical systems. Long-term, I hope to work in a company that values innovation, sustainability, and diversity — where I can use engineering to improve efficiency and create lasting impact.

As a first-generation student and Mexican-American woman in engineering, I aim to inspire others to chase their goals without fear. My journey is not just about reaching success, but paving the way for others to follow.

"I believe in building not just machines, but opportunities — for myself and those who come after me."

Jamelly Ramirez — October 2025
